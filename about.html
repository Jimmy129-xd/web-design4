Our Philosophy & Process

We aim to create a comfortable and expressive environment for every interviewee. Our typical workflow includes:

  1. Pre-interview conversation to understand background and themes.
  2. Minimal and natural lighting setup.
  3. Open-ended conversation paired with candid portrait captures.
  4. Post-processing focused on natural tones and emotional authenticity.
