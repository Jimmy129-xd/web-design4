Interview-First Portrait Photography

This project focuses on portrait interviews: creating photographs that are guided by conversation, comfort, and authenticity rather than heavy staging.

Workflow

  1. Pre-talk: learn the interviewee’s background and key themes.
  2. Setup: minimal lighting, calm space, simple framing.
  3. Conversation: open-ended questions, short pauses, candid moments.
  4. Post: natural tones, emotional realism, consistent black-and-white style.

Interview Highlight

“The most meaningful images happen between the questions.” — Interviewee
